HI-TOYZ


HI-Toyz is a renowned toy store that has been catering to toy enthusiasts since its establishment in 2019. We pride ourselves on offering an extensive collection of plastic model kits and figure statues, making us a go-to destination for hobbyists and collectors alike. With a dedicated focus on quality and variety, HI-Toyz has earned a special place in the hearts of local collectors who trust us to provide the latest releases and sought-after items.


At HI-Toyz, we understand the passion for collecting and the joy that comes from owning exceptional toys. That's why we meticulously curate our selection, handpicking toys from various brands to ensure a diverse range of options for our customers. As a trusted reseller, we have established strong partnerships with renowned brands such as Tamiya, Bandai, Mattel Toys, Mini-GT, and many others. This allows us to offer authentic products that meet the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.


In addition to being a leading toy retailer, HI-Toyz has evolved into an activity center for toy collectors. We organize regular events, workshops, and gatherings where enthusiasts can connect, share their passion, and expand their knowledge. Our goal is to foster a vibrant community that celebrates the artistry and creativity of toys.


When you visit HI-Toyz, you can expect a welcoming and immersive experience. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist you in finding the perfect addition to your collection or guide you through the latest releases. We strive to create a space where every visit sparks excitement and leaves you with cherished memories.


Discover the world of HI-Toyz and explore a captivating realm of toys and collectibles. Join us on this thrilling journey as we continue to bring joy, inspiration, and a touch of magic to toy enthusiasts of all ages.
